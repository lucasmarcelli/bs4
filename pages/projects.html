  • Game made for Game Design Class

    This is a game that myself and two other group members designed and implemented over the course of a semester! It's about controlling gravity to solve a platformer.

  • Global Offensive Case Simulator

    Not gonna lie, I've spent money on opening cases in Counter Strike. It's fun and it's a thrill to open a stattrak marble fade. We started making this simulator as a joke but as it shows, it's literally impossible to come out ahead unless you get very lucky. I know people who have spent thousands on cases and I hope that someone might see this and think twice before they drop real money on a 0.01% chance.

  • Mood Tracker

    A personal project of mine that might one day be helpful to others. It's a webapp to track your mood and other things over the day and identify problem times and habits. Tracking these things can really help mental health.
