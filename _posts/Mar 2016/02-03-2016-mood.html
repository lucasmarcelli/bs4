We wake up in a different mood than we went to bed as. Stuff happens in the day and we react, our plans go awry or we find $10 on the ground, and our day is different than it would have been if that hadn't happened.

Our bodies also have natural cycles in neurotransmitters and horomones. Our moods change that way too.

Tracking the way you feel over the day is actually super beneficial, and after you get a baseline it's possible to see how things like medicatio, diet and really any change are actually affecting your mood. As long as the self-reporting is done honestly it could have a huge effect on your overall mental health.

I started making this as an android app but I have decided to begin by creating a strong backend with django and hosting a webapp, and branching out to mobile apps after, in order to better showcase my full-stack developer skills. I don't really know django super well yet, so this will serve to teach me a lot about that!

The nice thing about doing this instead of using one of the apps availible is that I can make it exactly tailored to the needs I have. Hopefully someone else can get a bit of use out of it too.
